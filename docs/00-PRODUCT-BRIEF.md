# Product Brief — Morocco Livestock Platform V1

## Problem

Moroccan livestock farmers often track animals through notebooks, spreadsheets, memory, or messy paperwork. This makes it hard to track animal growth, health, sale readiness, and market availability. During high-demand periods such as Eid al-Adha, poor visibility into supply can amplify market stress.

## V1 product

A responsive web app where farmers can:

- register livestock,
- track weight and growth,
- track health records,
- upload animal photos,
- create public marketplace listings,
- receive buyer contact through phone or WhatsApp,
- discover verified vets.

Admins can:

- verify vets,
- moderate suspicious listings,
- suspend users/listings,
- view basic platform statistics.

## Primary users

| User | Need |
|---|---|
| Farmer | Manage animals and sell livestock faster |
| Public Buyer/Guest | Search livestock listings nationally with regional filters without a registered buyer account in V1 |
| Vet | Appear as a verified professional |
| Admin | Keep the platform clean and trusted |

## V1 success metric

A farmer can register, add at least one animal with a photo, create a listing, and receive buyer contact through phone/WhatsApp.

## Positioning

Do not call this a CRUD app. The correct positioning is:

> A livestock registry and market access platform for Moroccan farmers.
